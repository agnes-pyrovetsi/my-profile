My Passions and Interests

I have a wide range of passions that include architecture, F1 and racing cars, traveling, photography, and animals. Each of these has played a significant role in shaping who I am today, and I'm eager to dive deeper into each one.

Architecture

My passion for architecture is deeply rooted in my admiration for my mother, an architect whose work has inspired me since I was a little kid. Her dedication and creativity sparked my interest and led me to pursue it further. I attended weekly architectural design and free-hand drawing courses for two years during high school, enhancing my skills and understanding. Additionally, I've participated in several summer programs and online courses focused on the principles of architecture and its history through time, broadening my knowledge and my enthusiasm for it.

F1 and Racing Cars

Ever since I was little, I loved watching F1 races with my dad every Sunday. It was exciting and got me really into all the cool strategies and tech behind the races. Later, I got to dive even deeper into this world by joining the F1 in Schools competition. It was a chance to really get hands-on and learn how these fast cars work. Moving from just watching the races to actually being part of a racing competition made me love it even more.
